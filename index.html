5. Analysis Methodology

This analysis was generated by parsing 115 receipt markdown files across both academic years (2024-2025 and 2025-2026). A total of 197 individual line items were extracted and normalized into 146 unique products. Items were matched across receipts using name normalization to identify price trends.

11 items showed price differences across purchases. 19 items maintained stable pricing. The remaining items appeared only once and could not be compared.
